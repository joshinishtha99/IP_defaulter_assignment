Creamy Mushroom Risotto

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 8 oz mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable or chicken broth, heated
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet or saucepan, heat the olive oil and 1 tablespoon of butter over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and saut√© until translucent.
  2. Add the sliced mushrooms and cook until they release their moisture and turn golden brown. Remove half of the cooked mushrooms and set aside.
  3. Stir in the Arborio rice and minced garlic. Cook for a few minutes until the rice is lightly toasted.
  4. Pour in the white wine and cook, stirring constantly, until the wine is absorbed.
  5. Begin adding the heated broth, one ladleful at a time, stirring continuously and allowing each addition to be absorbed before adding more. Continue until the rice is creamy and cooked al dente.
  6. Stir in the reserved cooked mushrooms, grated parmesan cheese, and remaining tablespoon of butter. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  7. Remove from heat and let the risotto rest for a minute before serving.
  8. Serve the creamy mushroom risotto in bowls, garnished with fresh parsley.
